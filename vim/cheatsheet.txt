Macros:
- qa records macro to 'a'
- @a replays it
- "ap outputs it
- "ay saves the output after you edit

Movement:
{}              Next/previous empty line
()              Next/previous sentence
HML             Top/center/bottom of screen (currently inoperative)
*/#             Find next/previous instance of word
g*/g#           Same without word boundaries
[[, ]], [], ][  Go to opening and closing braces
''              Go to last mark
`               Goes to column AND row for mark
C               Replace to end of line
j               Joins line (currently inoperative)
